About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and thenl release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Resues is a member fo the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association

We Help many animals, such as:

  • Raccoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
